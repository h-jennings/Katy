Welcome.

I am a Cybersecurity and Privacy Professional in the Washington, DC area. Coming from a background in international relations, I've found that working in cybersecurity is a fascinating intersection between foreign policy, national security and human rights. I am always reading up on the latest trends and technologies in the field to see what new cybersecurity and privacy challenges we face. I firmly believe in resilience to data security incidents through war-gaming and Privacy by Design. Currently, I am a Cyber Risk Consultant for Deloitte, where I work to protect users and organizations through robust data governance solutions.

Explore this site to learn more about my experience and find out how to get in contact.

    Certification

    I am accredited as a Certified Information Privacy Professionals in US Private Sector Privacy (CIPP/US).

    Membership

    The IAPP is the largest and most comprehensive global information privacy community and resource. Founded in 2000, the IAPP is a not-for-profit organization that helps define, support and improve the privacy profession globally.

    Membership

    Women in CyberSecurity (WiCyS) is the premier organization with national reach dedicated to bringing together women in cybersecurity from academia, research and industry to share knowledge, experience, networking and mentoring.
